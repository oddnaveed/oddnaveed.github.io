# [Ali's revision techniques](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ukLnPbIffxE)

## Non-effective(having low utility) methods

- rereading
- highlighting (does little to boost performance)
- summarizing and making notes

## Effective method (pivotal methods)

active recall (practice testing)

### Anki flashcard

### making notes with the book closed

draw spider diagram

### write questions for yourself

## [spaced repetition](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z-zNHHpXoMM)

- study once, recall once, space it and repeat
- use spreadsheet(excel or googledocs) to record and manage revision dates. Color priorities (red, yellow, green)
- do not stick to timetable. Notice opportunity cost.
