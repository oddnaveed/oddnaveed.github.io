# Structure
## for one person
* This lecture was about TOPIC which explained HOW/WHAT/WHY
* S/He talked about how KEYWORD 1
* S/He mentioned that KEYWORD 2
* S/He discussed the way in which KEYWORD 3
* S/He highlighted KEYWORD 4
* S/He talked about KEYWORD 5
* S/He suggested that KEYWORD 6
also, in addition, although, finally, lastly
notice: we need to...  we should...   we must...  it is necessary to... we should understand...
<!--more-->
## for interviews(two people)
* The FIRST SPEAKER was discussing TOPIC
* S/He talked about KEYWORD 1
* S/He mentioned KEYWORD 2
* The SECOND SPEAKER discussed KEYWORD 3
* S/He highlighted KEYWORD 4
* S/He talked about KEYWORD 5
* S/He suggested that KEYWORD 6


This lecture was about **instruments of writing** which explained **how it can affect the style of a writier or perhaps level of production.** It was clearly mentioned that **the quill pen needed continuous refilling** and **in the 19th century, fountain pen was invented**. It was also mentioned that **the purpose of a typewriter was to speed up the writing process**. Overall, the purpose of the lecture was *...*(paraphrase the first sentence).
